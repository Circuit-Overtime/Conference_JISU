Objective of the Conference:

Foster collaboration and networking among professionals from academia, industry, and government sectors.

Facilitate the dissemination of cutting-edge research findings and innovative methodologies in operations research and business analytics.

Promote interdisciplinary approaches and cross-disciplinary collaborations to address complex real-world problems.

Provide a forum for discussing emerging trends, best practices, and future directions in the field.

Encourage the integration of theoretical insights with practical applications to enhance decision-making processes and organizational performance.
