About Us:

About the Conference

The International Conference on Next-Generation Technologies and Computing (ICNGTC-2025) is a leading global platform dedicated to exploring groundbreaking innovations and research in advanced computing, artificial intelligence (AI), wireless communication, cybersecurity, and emerging digital technologies. This conference brings together academicians, researchers, industry experts, and policymakers to discuss the latest advancements, challenges, and future directions in next-generation computing and communication systems.

In an era where AI-driven automation, high-performance computing, 6G networks, and quantum computing are redefining industries, ICNGTC serves as a critical hub for fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge sharing. The conference aims to bridge the gap between academia and industry, providing a space where innovative research meets real-world applications. ICNGTC promotes discussions on cutting-edge technologies, such as machine learning, blockchain, IoT, neuromorphic computing, and cybersecurity frameworks, which are shaping the future of digital transformation. It also encourages debates on ethical AI, responsible computing, and sustainability in next-generation systems, ensuring that technological advancements align with societal needs.

The conference will feature a diverse set of sessions, including keynote addresses by world- renowned scientists, panel discussions with industry leaders, technical paper presentations, and hands-on workshops. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in networking sessions, innovation challenges, and startup showcases, providing a holistic experience for both research and commercial collaboration. By fostering knowledge exchange, cross-sector partnerships, and forward-thinking discussions, ICNGTC aims to shape the future of computing and communication, making it an essential event for those passionate about pioneering the next generation of technological breakthroughs.

Call for Paper

All honorable authors are encouraged to contribute original research papers for ICNGTC-2025 on the following (but not limited to) research topics:

1. Advanced Computing & AI
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning
  • Edge and Cloud Computing for Intelligent Systems
  • High-Performance Computing (HPC) and Quantum Computing
  • Explainable AI and Ethical Considerations
2. Communication & Networking
  • 6G Wireless Networks and Beyond
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Connectivity
  • Software-Defined Networks (SDN) and Network Function Virtualization (NFV)
  • Blockchain and Secure Communications
3. Cybersecurity & Privacy
  • AI-Driven Cybersecurity Threat Detection
  • Privacy-Preserving Computing and Federated Learning
  • Post-Quantum Cryptography
  • Zero-Trust Security Frameworks
4. Emerging Technologies & Applications
  • Digital Twins and Augmented Reality (AR)/Virtual Reality (VR)
  • Neuromorphic and Bio-Inspired Computing
  • Sustainable and Green Computing Technologies
  • Smart Cities and Industry 4.0

Publication

Taylor and Francis (T&F) constitute a prominent global publishing enterprise that is committed to rendering high-caliber research comprehensible and accessible to individuals worldwide, thereby facilitating the convergence of diverse individuals, concepts, and perspectives to disseminate the insights of experts, theoretical frameworks, and empirical truths. All full-length papers that receive acceptance at the ICNGTC-2025 Conference will be compiled in a conference proceeding published by Taylor and Francis (T&F) in both print and electronic formats, each possessing an ISBN for the book and a DOI for each respective chapter, along with promotional activities on the T&F website. T&F will advocate for the published volume to be considered for indexing by Scopus, contingent upon adherence to Scopus's selection criteria and the fulfillment of the scope and quality standards requisite for Scopus Indexing.
